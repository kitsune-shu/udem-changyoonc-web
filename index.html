I am a graduate student working in mathematical and computational biology, focusing on stochastic modeling of gene regulatory systems and the mechanisms that allow biological processes to remain robust in noisy environments.

Previously, I studied 3D animation and visual effects in the United States, and later moved to Canada to study mathematics. I enjoy interdisciplinary work across mathematics, biology, and data science.
